Campus Life

the school church

Was established in 1978

The church of Don Bosco School, Siliguri is a historic shrine. Daily the christian students visit the church.

school library

Was established in 1980

The library is very large and flourishes in all types of books. Books of all the subjects are available. Story books, detective books, biographies, autobiographies and even encyclopedias are also available.

school auditorium

Was established in 1985

The auditorium is a spacious one with more than a capacity of 800. Most of the school programs are held here.

Awarded the best boys school

Don Bosco School was awarded the BEST BOYS SCHOOL AWARD IN 2019.

Don Bosco School has a history of polishing the students to be dutiful sons of their motherland. It was due to this culture of DBS, Siliguri, that it was awarded "THE BEST BOYS' DAY SCHOOL IN ASIA", by the ASIA EDUCATION AWARDS, 2019.

Ranked no. 1 in W.B. & India

Don Bosco School, Siliguri topped in W.B. & India as the No. 1 Boys Day School

Don Bosco School, Siliguri, has ranked NO.1 in West Bengal, Siliguri, and in India. It has been awarded "EDUCATION TODAY INDIA SCHOOL MERIT AWARDS 2019-2020" in INDIA'S TOP 5 BOYS DAY SCHOOL category.
